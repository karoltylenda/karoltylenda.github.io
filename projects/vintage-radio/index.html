Receiving

This is how you can receive Vintage Radio

APPS
Mobile Apps
Apple TV

Receive Vintage Radio 80s directly on Apple TV in the Vintage Radio tvOS app.

Apple Watch

Install the Vintage Radio App on the iPhone and start the app with your Apple Watch. Or download the Vintage Radio app directly from the Apple Watch Store.

Car Play

Install the Vintage Radio app on the iPhone and start the app with your system in the car.

Desktop

Listen to vintage radio directly in your browser via the URL 80s.vintageradio.ch. We recommend the latest version of Google Chrome.

Radio

Listen to Vintage Radio 80s directly from your web radio. Use the following stream URLs:

128kbit/s: https://vintage80s.ice.infomaniak.ch/vintage80s-high.mp3

64kbit/s: https://vintage80s.ice.infomaniak.ch/vintage80s-low.mp3

Smartspeaker
Amazon Alexa

Say: «Alexa, play Vintage Radio Switzerland».

Google Home

Say: «Hey Google, play vintageradio.ch».
